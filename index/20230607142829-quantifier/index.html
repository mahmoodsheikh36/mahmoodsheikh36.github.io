quantifier

a quantifier is an operator that specifies how many individuals in the domain of discourse satisfy a formula

examples of quantifiers:

  • universal quantifier
  • existential quantifier

quantifiers can come next to each other or they can be moved around in a correct way that also preserves their order in the formula, see https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prenex_normal_form
