\begin{question}
there are 2 traffic lights, the probability of an error in the first is 0.1, second 0.15, both 0.02, whats the probability of no errors in either\\
\begin{answer}
A - error in first\\
B - error in second\\
\(P(A \cap B)=0.02\)\\
\(P(A \cup B)=P(A)+P(B)-P(A \cap B)\)\\
\end{answer}
\end{question}

\begin{subquestion}
given that the student has passed an exam, whats the probability that its a multiple choice questions exam\\
\begin{answer}
A - multiple choice questions exam\\
B - passed exam\\
\(P(A|B)=\frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}=\frac{P(A)P(B|A)}{P(B)}\)\\
we need to be build a \href{20230126225225-probability_tree.org}{probability tree}\\

\begin{question}

\begin{answer}
X - women picked\\

\begin{question}
a professor has 8 keys of which only one opens a given lock, he tries the keys at random, but doesnt mark a key after trying it, whats the probability that it takes him more than 8 attempts to find the correct key? define a \href{20230104221353-random_variable.org}{random variable} and find its distribution in the process\\
\begin{answer}
\href{20230113173836-geometric_distribution.org}{geometric distribution}\\
\(X \sim G(1/8)\)\\
the answer is \(1-\sum_{k=1}^{8} P(X=k)\)\\
\end{answer}
\end{question}
