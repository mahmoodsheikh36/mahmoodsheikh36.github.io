to analyze the \href{20221130014441-time_complexity.org}{time complexity} of \href{20221105001640-recursive_function.org}{recursive function}s we use a mathematical concept called \textbf{recurrence relation}\\[0pt]
a recurrence defines \(T(n)\) in terms of \(T\) for smaller values\\[0pt]
\begin{my_example}
\(T(n) = T(n-1) + 1\), here \(T(n)\) is defined in terms of \(T(n-1)\)\\[0pt]
\end{my_example}
