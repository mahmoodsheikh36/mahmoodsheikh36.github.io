homework on turning natural language to propositional logic statements\\
\begin{question}
D - doctors stop their strike\\
H - hospital managements invervene\\
P - court intervenes\\
C - the health of patients doesnt get harmed\\
M - the government increases the budget\\
turn the following sentences into logical \href{20230401175139-statement.org}{statement}s\\
\begin{enumerate}
\item if the doctors dont stop their strike hospital managements invervene\\
\item if the health of patients doesnt get harmed the government doesnt increase the budget\\
\item if hospital managements invervene then the health of patients doesnt get harmed or the court intervenes\\
\item the court doesnt intervene and the goverment doesnt increase the budget\\
\end{enumerate}
\begin{answer}
\begin{enumerate}
\item \(\lnot D \implies H\)\\
\item \(C \implies \lnot M\)\\
\item \(H \implies (C \lor P)\)\\
\item \(\lnot P \land \lnot M\)\\
\end{enumerate}
\end{answer}
\end{question}

\end{answer}
\end{question}
