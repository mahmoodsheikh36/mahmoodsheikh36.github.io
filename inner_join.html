inner join

for a NATURAL JOIN operation , only tuples from that have matching tuples in –and vice versa–appear in the result. hence, tuples without a matching (or related) tuple are eliminated from the JOIN result. tuples with NULL values in the join attributes are also eliminated. this type of join, where tuples with no match are eliminated, is known as an inner join.

a JOIN in sql defaults to INNER JOIN
