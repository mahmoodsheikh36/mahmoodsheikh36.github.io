the second factor in tf-idf is used to give a higher weight to words that occur only in a few documents. terms that are limited to a few documents are useful for discriminating those documents from the rest of the collection; terms that occur frequently across the entire collection aren't as helpful. the document frequency of a term is the number of documents it occurs in. document frequency is not the same as the collection frequency of a term, which is the total number of times the word appears in the whole collection in any document.
