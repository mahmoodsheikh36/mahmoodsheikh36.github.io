### <span class="section-num">1.5</span> dsbm {#dsbm}

**DSBM** is short for **depressive suicidal black metal**, which is an extreme metal genre idealizing suicidal thoughts and self harm <br/>
dsbm is like a drug to me, the more i listen to it the more brutal i need it to be, what was once depressing enough is just not enough anymore and i have to find something even more extreme, too bad this cycle cant go on for too long as i've already explored most of the genre <br/>
