given the real matrix
\[ A = \pmqty{-1 & -4 & -4\\ 2 & 5 & 4\\ -2 & -4 & -3} \]

find the characteristic polynomial and the eigenvalues of \(A\)

\begin{align*} p(\lambda) &= \left|\pmqty{-1 & -4 & -4\\ 2 & 5 & 4\\ -2 & -4 & -3} - \pmqty{\lambda & 0 & 0\\ 0 & \lambda & 0\\ 0 & 0 & \lambda}\right|\\ &= \vmqty{-1-\lambda & -4 & -4\\ 2 & 5-\lambda & 4\\ -2 & -4 & -3-\lambda}\\ &= (-1-\lambda) \cdot (5-\lambda) \cdot (-3-\lambda) + -4 \cdot 4 \cdot -2 + -4 \cdot 2 \cdot -4 - -2 \cdot (5 - \lambda) \cdot -4 - -4 \cdot 4 \cdot (-1 - \lambda) - (-3 - \lambda) \cdot 2 \cdot -4\\ &= -\lambda^3 + \lambda^2 + \lambda - 1\\ &= (\lambda-1)(1-\lambda^2)\\ &= (\lambda-1)(1-\lambda)(1+\lambda) \end{align*}

and so the eigenvalues are \(\lambda=1,1,-1\) and the characteristic polynomial is \(p(\lambda) = -\lambda^3 + \lambda^2 + \lambda - 1\)

find the bases of the eigenspaces of \(A\)

to find the eigenspaces we find the eigenvectors
we substitute \(\lambda = 1\)

\begin{gather*} \lambda = 1 \implies \pmqty{-1-1 & -4 & -4\\ 2 & 5-1 & 4\\ -2 & -4 & -3-1} \implies \pmqty{1 & 2 & 2\\ 0 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 0}\\ x = -2y-2z\\ \bmqty{-2y-2z\\ y\\ z} = y\bmqty{-2\\ 1\\ 0} + z\bmqty{-2\\ 0\\ 1} \end{gather*}

so the eigenvalue \(\lambda = 1\) has the corresponding eigenvectors \(\bmqty{-2\\ 1\\ 0},\bmqty{-2\\ 0\\ 1}\) and the corresponding eigenspace \(\spn\left(\left\{\bmqty{-2\\ 1\\ 0},\bmqty{-2\\ 0\\ 1}\right\}\right)\)
we substitute \(\lambda = -1\)

\begin{gather*} -1 = -1 \implies \pmqty{-1+1 & -4 & -4\\ 2 & 5+1 & 4\\ -2 & -4 & -3+1} \implies \pmqty{1 & 0 & -1\\ 0 & 1 & 1\\ 0 & 0 & 0}\\ x=z,y=-z\\ \bmqty{z\\ -z\\ z} = z\bmqty{1\\ -1\\ 1} \end{gather*}

so the eigenvalue \(\lambda = -1\) has the corresponding eigenvector \(\bmqty{1\\ -1\\ 1}\) and the corresponding eigenspace \(\spn\left(\left\{\bmqty{1\\ -1\\ 1}\right\}\right)\)

find the eigenbasis matrix of \(A\) and find \(A^{2021}\)

\begin{gather*} T = \pmqty{-2 & -2 & 1\\ 1 & 0 & -1\\ 0 & 1 & 1}\\ T^{-1}AT = \pmqty{1 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & -1}\\ A = T\pmqty{1 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & -1}T^{-1}\\ A^{2020} = T\pmqty{1^{2020} & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 1^{2020} & 0\\ 0 & 0 & (-1)^{2020}}T^{-1} = \pmqty{1 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & 1}\\ A^{2021} = T\pmqty{1^{2020} & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 1^{2020} & 0\\ 0 & 0 & (-1)^{2020}}T^{-1}T\pmqty{1 & 0 & 0\\ 0 & 1 & 0\\ 0 & 0 & -1}T^{-1} = A \end{gather*}
