hypergeometric distribution

a probability distribution
out of \(n\) different items of which \(m\) are of a specific type and \(n-m\) are of another type, we pick \(k\) items without repetition, the probability that from \(k\) items \(r\) are of the first type and \(k-r\) are of the second type is:
\[ P_n(k) = \frac{\binom{m}{r} \binom{n-m}{k-r}}{\binom{n}{k}} \]

  • the symbol of the hypergeometric distribution is \(X \sim H(k,m,n)\)
  • expected value is \(\mathrm{E}(x)=n \cdot \frac{R}{N}\)
  • variance is \(\mathrm{Var}(x)=\frac{k \cdot m \cdot (n-m) \cdot (n-k)}{n^2 \cdot (n-1)}\)
