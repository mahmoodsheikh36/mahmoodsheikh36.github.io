joint probability distribution

given two random variables that are defined on the same probability space, the joint probability distribution is the corresponding probability distribution on all possible pairs of outputs. it is denoted by \(P(X=x,Y=y)\)

\[ P(X=x,Y=y) \geq 0 \]

\[ \sum_{x} \sum_{y} P(X=x,Y=y)=1 \]
