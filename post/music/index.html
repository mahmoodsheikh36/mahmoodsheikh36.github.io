1.5 dsbm

DSBM is short for depressive suicidal black metal, which is an extreme metal genre idealizing suicidal thoughts and self harm
dsbm is like a drug to me, the more i listen to it the more brutal i need it to be, what was once depressing enough is just not enough anymore and i have to find something even more extreme, too bad this cycle cant go on for too long as i’ve already explored most of the genre

1.5.1 Forgotten Tomb

this band is my favorite in the dsbm scene, their track disheartenment will make your ears bleed

Songs to Leave - Forgotten Tomb


1.5.2 Psychonaut 4

the most iconic band in this scene

Dispomania - Psychonaut 4
