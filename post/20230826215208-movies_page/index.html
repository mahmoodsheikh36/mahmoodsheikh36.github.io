these movies are very extreme, as there is alot of sadism, perversion and inhumanity (not all of them are this extreme though, e.g. Pan’s Labyrinth isnt)
i even i had to remove some entries from this section because i didnt wanna go too far
that said, these movies arent purely for shock value, if thats what i wanted i wouldve gone for the likes of the appaling A Serbian Film and Cannibal Holocaust, but those are just edgie, gross and dull
