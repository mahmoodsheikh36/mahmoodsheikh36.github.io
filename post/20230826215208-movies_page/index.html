these movies are very extreme, as there is alot of sadism, perversion and inhumanity

that said, these movies arent purely for shock value, if thats what i wanted i wouldve gone for the likes of the appalling A Serbian Film and Cannibal Holocaust, but those are just edgy, gross and dull
