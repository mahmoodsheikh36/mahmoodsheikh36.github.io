given the linear operator \(L: M_2(\mathbb{R}) \to M_2(\mathbb{R})\) defined by the formula \(L(X) = XA + BX\) such that \(A = \begin{pmatrix}1 & 1\\ 1 & 1\end{pmatrix}, B = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & -1\\ -1 & 1 \end{pmatrix}\)

find the matrix of \(L\) with the basis \(E_1 = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0\\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix}, E_2 = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1\\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix}, E_3 = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0\\ 0 & -1 \end{pmatrix}, E_4 = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1\\ -1 & 0 \end{pmatrix}\) of \(M_2(\mathbb{R})\)

find bases of \(\ker L, \img L\)

given the linear operator \(L: M_2(\mathbb{R}) \to M_2(\mathbb{R})\) defined by the formula \(L(X) = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 \end{pmatrix}X + X\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1\\ 1 & 1 \end{pmatrix}\)

find the matrix of \(L\) in the basis \(B_1=\begin{pmatrix} 1 & -1\\ 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}, B_2=\begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1\\ 0 & -1 \end{pmatrix}, B_3=\begin{pmatrix} 0 & 0\\ -1 & 1 \end{pmatrix}, B_4=\begin{pmatrix} 0 & 0\\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix}\)

find the bases of \(\ker L, \img L\)

given the matrix \(B=\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2\\ 1 & 1 \end{pmatrix}\), the transformation \(T: M_{2\times2}(\mathbb{R}) \to M_{2\times2}(\mathbb{R})\) defined by \(T(A)=BA\) such that \(A \in M_{2\times2}(\mathbb{R})\)

check whether \(T\) is a linear transformation over \(M_{2\times2}(\mathbb{R})\)

find the basis and dimension of the kernel and image of \(T\)

given the linear transformation \(L: M_2(\mathbb{R}) \to M_2(\mathbb{R})\) defined by \(L \left(\begin{pmatrix} x & y\\ z & w \end{pmatrix}\right) = \begin{pmatrix} x & y\\ z & w \end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2\\ 3 & 6 \end{pmatrix}\)

find \(\dim\ker L, \dim\img L\)
