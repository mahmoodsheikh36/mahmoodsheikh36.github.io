emacs pinky

i know exactly how it feels when your pinky (leftmost finger) hurts from pressing the left control key often when using emacs
to solve this i decided to map the right alt key to the ctrl key, to do that on linux i used the following command

setxkbmap -option ctrl:ralt_rctrl

you can put this line in your ~/.zprofile or ~/.xinitrc and youll be fine
to press the right alt key (which is now mapped to ctrl) i use my right thumb, problem solved.
